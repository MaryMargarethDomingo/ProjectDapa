During Volcanic Eruption

1. Be aware of mudflows

- The danger from a mudflow increases near stream channels and with prolonged heavy rains.

 
2. Stay out

- Stay out of designated restricted zones. Effects of a volcanic eruption can be experienced many miles from a volcano.

          

If you are caught in an ashfall:

1. Wear a dust mask designed to protect against lung irritation from small particles.
2. Keep as much of your skin covered as possible.
3. Stay out of designated restricted zones.
4. Protect your eyes by wearing goggles. Wear eyeglasses, not contact lenses.
