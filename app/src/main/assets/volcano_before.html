How To Be Prepared and Aware Before A Volcano Errupts?

1. Have an Emergency Kit Ready

- Maintain emergency food, water and other supplies, including a flashlight, a portable battery-operated radio, extra batteries, medicines, first aid kit and clothing

2. Be Aware
  

- Each one should be aware of the dangers that volcanic eruptions pose to lives and be prepared to face whatever circumstances the eruption may bring

3. Make a Family Emergency Plan

- Your family may not be together when disaster strikes, so it is important to know how you will contact one another, how you will get back together and what you will do in case of an emergency

4. Find out about the volcanic risk in your community
 

- Ask your local council about emergency plans and how they will warn you of a volcanic eruption.
