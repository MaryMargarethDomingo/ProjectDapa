What To Do During A Flood?

If A Flood Is Likely In Your Area, You Should:

1. Listen to the radio or television for information
 
2. Be aware that flash flooding can occur
 
 
3. Monitor your surroundings
 
4. Be aware of stream, drainage channels, canyons and other areas known to flood suddenly
          

If You Must Prepare To Evacuate, You Should Do The Following:

  
1. Secure your home
 
2. Turn off utilities at the main switches or valves if instructed to do so
          

If You Have To Leave Your Home, Remember These Evacuation Tips:

  
1. Do not walk through moving water
 
2. Do not drive into flooded areas
