During Flood

1. Stay inside the house
 
 
2. Always keep yourself updated with the latest weather report.
          

If There Is A Need To Move To An Evacuation Center, Follow These Reminders:

1. Evacuate calmly
 
2. Avoid the way leading to the river.
3. Put important appliances and belongings in a high ground
4. Close the windows and turn off the main power switch
