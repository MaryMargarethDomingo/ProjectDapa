How To Be Prepared and Aware Before A Typhoon Hits?

1. Have an Emergency Kit Ready

- Maintain emergency food, water and other supplies, including a flashlight, a portable battery-operated radio, extra batteries, medicines, first aid kit and clothing

2. Have a Disaster Plan
  

-Decide how and where your family will reunite if separated. Prepare yourself and your home by completing the activities on this checklist.

3. Stay updated on the weather reports
4. Examine your house and repair its unstable parts.
