During Fire


1. Get to the nearest exit immediately

- If there’s smoke in the air, get as low as possible where you may be able to breathe and feel your way out

2. Once you’re out, contact emergency services.

- Call the fire department or head to a neighbor’s house to ask them to call.

3. Do not use a doorways

- Do not use a doorway except if you know it is a strongly supported, load-bearing doorway and it is close to you. Many inside doorways are lightly constructed and do not offer protection.

4. Never use an elevator during a fire
 

- Always take the stairs.
