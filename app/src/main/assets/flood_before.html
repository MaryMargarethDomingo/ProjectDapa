How To Be Prepared and Aware Before A Flood Hits?

1. Listen to the radio or television for information

- Turn On your Radio for information and updates on the flood.

2. Be Alert

- Monitor your surroundings, local television and radio stations.

- Check local sites (NDRRMC)

3.Review your Family Disaster Plan

- Discuss flood plans with your family.

4. Be prepared to evacuate

- Identify alternative travel routes that are not prone to flooding

5. Keep a List of Emergency Contacts
 

- Designate a contact person who can be reached if family members get separated. Make sure every family member has the contact information

6. If a flash flood warning is issued for your area: Climb to safety immediately.
 

- Get out of low areas subject to flooding.
